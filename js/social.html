Cloths Donation Drive

A collection of old clothes was collected from students of NIT-Trichy and donated to the orphanage. This drive was conducted successfully and many clothing items were donated to orphanages. This prevented the old clothes from being wasted by putting them to good use.
We have found that among its other benefits, giving liberates the giver's soul.

Blood Donation Camp

NCC is not just for parades and camps. We all have our social responsibilities and duties for our motherland. As part of our social responsibilities, we, the cadets of A Flight NCC, have conducted blood donation camps on our campus to encourage many students to join us in this social responsibility. Blood donation is not just a social event or an act of kindness. It has its benefits for the person who does that too.

Orphanage Vist

We conduct orphanage visits once every two semesters. Every time we visit the orphanage, we conduct skits and present a musical show for them.

Eye CheckUp Camp

We conduct free eye checkups for the students and staff so that they can keep a constant check of their eye powers without the need for traveling to the city and getting it done for a large sum.

Candlelight vigil

We conducted a candlelight vigil in honor of General Bipin Rawat after he suddenly passed away in a helicopter crash. As a mark of our respect for him and his patriotism, the whole college participated in the candlelight vigil.
